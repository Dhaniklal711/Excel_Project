This project presents a comprehensive **Excel-based Sales & Business Performance Dashboard** designed to analyze order trends, regional performance, and overall business metrics across multiple states and union territories in India.

The dashboard provides end-to-end insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency using advanced Excel features such as Pivot Tables, data visualization, and statistical analysis.

🔍 Key Highlights:
• Analyzed data across **36 States & Union Territories** with over **31,000+ orders**
• Identified **Top 5 and Bottom 5 performing regions** for targeted decision-making
• Calculated key statistical measures including **mean, median, mode, range, max & min**
• Evaluated **business scale metrics** like revenue, average order value, and units sold
• Built **market penetration analysis** to compare above and below average performing regions
• Conducted **delivery performance analysis** (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
• Performed **quarter-on-quarter growth analysis** highlighting declining trends and business risks
• Developed **growth trajectory and deceleration insights** for strategic planning

📈 Tools & Techniques Used:
• Microsoft Excel
• Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
• Data Cleaning & Transformation
• Statistical Analysis
• Data Visualization & Dashboard Design

🎯 Business Impact:
This dashboard helps stakeholders understand performance gaps, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions to improve market expansion, operational efficiency, and revenue growth.

🚀 Conclusion:
The project demonstrates how Excel can be effectively used as a powerful business intelligence tool to transform raw data into meaningful insights and actionable strategies.
